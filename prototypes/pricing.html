Frequently asked questions

What map data does MapPoster use?

We use Google's Photorealistic 3D Tiles — the same technology behind Google Earth. Every location is rendered with real satellite imagery and 3D building models.

Can I use my posters commercially?

Free tier posters include a small watermark and are for personal use. Pro subscribers get watermark-free exports suitable for commercial use, gifts, and print sales.

What resolution are the exports?

Free exports are screen resolution (1x). Pro exports support up to 4K+ resolution with print-ready DPI settings and bleed marks for professional printing.

How do AI art styles work?

We use Google's Gemini vision models to transform your 3D map render into artistic styles — watercolor, oil painting, anime, blueprint, and more. The AI processes your exact camera view.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No lock-in, no hidden fees.
