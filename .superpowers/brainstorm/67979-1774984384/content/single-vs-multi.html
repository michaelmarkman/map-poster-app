The question:

A

v1 = Single Pin only

Ship fast with single-location posters. Multi-pin is the big v2 feature that brings people back. Covers most use cases (Our Spot, First Home, Where We Met, Trip Memento).

B

v1 = Both, multi-pin is basic

Ship single-pin polished + a simpler multi-pin (max 4–5 pins, auto-layout, no route lines). Good enough to differentiate, save the fancy stuff for v2.

C

v1 = Multi-pin is the hero

Lead with multi-pin as the differentiator. Single-pin is just multi-pin with one pin. More ambitious, longer to build, but competitors don't have this.
